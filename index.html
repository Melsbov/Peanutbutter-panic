Info

The objective of this game is to get a slice of bread and put peanutbutter on it.
There are 3 modes: Normal, Timed and Random Selection. On normal mode you need to select one of the 4 items: Clip, Bread, Knife and lid. Once one of the items is selected you can move them with wasd or the arrow keys. On normal mode its the objective to use a little moves as possible. On timed mode its the same as Normal but you need to do it as fast as possible. Random Selection on the other hand is quite different, you cannot select an item yourself. It will be selected for you at random. You then need to, in as little moves as possible, also get a slice of bread with peanutbutter.

Congratulations you won!
